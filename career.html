Product Marketing Manager

About the company

Botsuite is a global chatbot marketing automation platform. We are helping more than 20,000 businesses across 20 countries engage and support millions of their customers in real time and at scale. Our goal is to disrupt the $5B email marketing industry by bringing the convenience of messenger products from our personal lives into business-to-customer communication.

About the Role

In this role you will be acting as the bridge between our product strategy and marketing initiatives that drive results. You will do the research, develop personas, customer journey and closely partner with our content team on creating resources that acquire, nurture and retain customers in the lifecycle. You will be preparing the GTM strategy, partner-up and communicate with our product team to ensure we are providing a seamless and amazing user experience from our marketing experience to our product.

Role and Responsibilities
  • A thriving relationship with your Product partners where you are a key resource in strategic decisions and together you are building an intuitive product experience that ensures customers can easily onboard and succeed with Botsuite
  • Creation of personas, customer journeys, positioning and messaging for our target segments.
  • Development and implementation of a brand-level product naming architecture that drives adoption and product comprehension.
  • Development of measurable GTM plans that are flawlessly executed to bring Botsuite product launches for our target audiences.
  • Creation of compelling education resources that address specific customer needs along their customer journey, including a brand new video course, lifecycle campaigns, case studies, and in-product content.
  • Completion of industry and competitive analysis that results in benchmarks, actionable marketing strategies, and product recommendations.
  • A product partnerships strategy complete with standardized rules of engagement and co-marketing paybooks that you and the rest of the Product Marketing team will leverage as we collaborate with our strategic partners.

Require Skills and Experience

  • 4+ years of continuous experience in Product Marketing, ideally with a self-serve SaaS product.
  • Command of the product marketing discipline, including user research, measurement, planning, crafting positioning/messaging, user product education, and developing creative assets (e.g., website content, videos, presentations).
  • Record of developing marketing strategies that deepen user engagement.
  • Comfort with ambiguity and have a proven ability to make informed decisions when the answers aren't immediately clear.
  • Ability to think strategically about complex issues leading to thoughtful recommendations and action plans.
  • Ability to successfully build relationships with global teams across geographical and cultural dependencies.
